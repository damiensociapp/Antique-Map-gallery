The Gallery

A trade passed down through generations

Antique Maps Gallery was founded on a simple inheritance: a lifelong love of the printed world. Raised among the shelves of two antiquarian dealers with more than forty years in rare books and maps, we know these objects intimately — where they were made, how they were printed, and why they still matter.

Today the gallery brings that expertise online, pairing serious collectors and first-time buyers alike with authentic pieces from the 15th to the 19th century. Every map we sell is one we would be proud to hang on our own wall.

Lisette Hoepelman
